# WPMS Release 1.1 — Customer Portal Foundation

## Added
- Staff-side Customer Portal workspace
- Customer portal invitation and access-status management
- Customer dashboard preview
- Pet, appointment, boarding, and balance summaries
- Demo-mode portal access persistence
- Supabase customer portal access migration
- Client Portal navigation item

## Test checklist
1. Open Customer Portal.
2. Select a customer and verify their pet and appointment summaries.
3. Invite the selected customer.
4. Change portal status between Invited, Active, and Suspended.
5. Refresh and confirm persistence.
6. Search portal accounts by name or email.
7. Verify responsive layout.
