# WPMS Release 1.7 — Memberships & Loyalty

## Added
- Membership plan catalog
- Customer enrollment and status management
- Monthly, quarterly, and annual billing frequencies
- Grooming and boarding discounts
- Included baths and nail trims
- Loyalty points and multipliers
- Renewal tracking
- Demo-mode persistence
- Supabase-ready membership tables

## Test checklist
1. Open Memberships.
2. Enroll a customer in a plan.
3. Edit status, renewal date, and points.
4. Search by customer, plan, or status.
5. Refresh and verify persistence.
